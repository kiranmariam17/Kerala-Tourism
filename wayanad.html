WAYANAD

Wayanad is situated in the north-east of Kerala with administrative headquarters at the municipality of Kalpetta. It came into existence on 1st November, 1980 consisting of Mananthavady, Sulthanbathery and Vythiri Taluks. Wayanad district is bordered by Karnataka to north and north-east, Tamil Nadu to the south-east, Malappuram to south, Kozhikode to the south-west and Kannur to the north-west. Wayanad, the green paradise is nestled among the mountains of the Western Ghats, forming the border world of the greener part of Kerala. Clean and pristine, enchanting and hypnotizing, this land is filled with history and culture.
